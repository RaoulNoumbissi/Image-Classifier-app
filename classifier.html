Machine Learning Classification Model

This app uses transfer learning to classify image data from a webcam.

Train your model by placing your webcam images into one of A, B or C image categories. Hold an image in front of the camera and press one of the class buttons below.

Make predictions after your model is trained. Hold your test image in front of the webcam and see which class the model thinks your image belongs to and its probability.
